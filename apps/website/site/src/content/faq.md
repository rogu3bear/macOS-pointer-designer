# FAQ
## What permissions does WindowDrop need?
WindowDrop requires macOS Accessibility permission so it can move windows.
## Does WindowDrop read my files or browsing?
No. It moves windows. It does not read window contents.
## Why do some dialogs not move?
Dialogs and sheets are intentionally ignored to avoid breaking workflows.
## Which apps are supported?
Finder, Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Terminal, Mail, Xcode, VS Code, and 20+ more across browsers, terminals, mail clients, and IDEs. Enable or disable apps in WindowDrop settings.
